## 4. Taking the Exam from Home (Remote Proctoring)

It is possible to take the exam remotely, but it has **strict requirements**.

Important conditions:

- No background programs should be running.
- Even default background applications may cause **automatic disqualification**.
- It is recommended to use a **separate guest OS account** for the exam environment.

Environment requirements:

- Webcam must be active.
- The **entire room must be shown clearly**.
- No study material should be within reach.
- You **cannot leave the seat or move around the room**.
- No one else should enter the room during the exam.

Monitoring:

- The system continuously monitors the **computer, candidate, and room environment**.
- Monitoring is done by a **human proctor together with AI software**.

Failure to follow any rule may result in:

- **Immediate disqualification**
- **Voucher expiration without appeal**

Because of these constraints, the **home exam option can be risky if not prepared properly**.
